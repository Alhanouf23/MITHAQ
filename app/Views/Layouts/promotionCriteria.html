Our Promotion Criteria

Performance Rating

Employees with a previous year rating of 4 or 5 are eligible for promotion consideration.

Training Engagement and Scores

Completion of at least 2 training programs in the previous year with a score of 80% or higher.

Experience

A minimum of 5 years of service.

Educational Background

Employees with higher education levels (e.g., Bachelor’s or Master’s) are prioritized for roles requiring advanced knowledge.

Specific Needs

Promotions are also influenced by the needs of the department.

Equity and Fairness

Factors such as gender or recruitment channel will not affect promotion decisions, ensuring a fair process.
